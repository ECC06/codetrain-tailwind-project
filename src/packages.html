Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right plan for me?

We recommend starting with a consultation to discuss your fitness goals and preferences. This way, we can tailor a plan that suits your individual needs.

What if I miss a scheduled training session?

If you miss a scheduled training session, please contact your trainer as soon as possible to discuss rescheduling options. We understand that life can be unpredictable, and we are here to help you stay on track with your fitness goals.

Can I switch my plan later?

Yes, you can switch your plan at any time. We understand that your needs may change, and we are flexible in accommodating those changes to ensure you have the best experience.

How do I contact my trainer for support?

You can contact your trainer for support by sending them an email or a message through the training app. They are available to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have.
